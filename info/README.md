## Tour Navigator - The Privacy-Friendly Hiking App

### What does the Tour Navigator app do?

It helps you perfectly plan every hike in advance and enjoy a relaxed experience on the trail.

The app creates a clear itinerary from an existing GPX file – with realistic walking times, split times, and a well-structured route, just as a tour guide would want for an ideal day of hiking.

### What makes the app privacy-friendly?

**No personal data** and **no GPX tracks** are stored or shared.

### What permissions does the app require?


### Only the following is required:

- Access to your **precise GPS location** (navigation & time management)

Optional (only when using corresponding features that you have previously enabled):

- Internet access for OpenStreetMap POIs, Wikipedia articles, and the Black Forest Association tour portal

- Route planning with Google Maps

### Real-time Navigation

During your tour, the app accompanies you in real time:

- Display of upcoming waypoints with planned and estimated arrival times

- Time management
- Warning if you leave the route (optional voice alert)

This requires GPS location services to be enabled with precise position data.

### What the app doesn't do

Tour Navigator deliberately **does not calculate alternative routes**, but guides you reliably along the planned track.
